Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Boom - Space Reflex respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected and how it is used when you play the game.

Information We Collect

The game may collect basic gameplay information such as player name, score, level, and leaderboard entries when you submit your score.

Firebase Services

The game may use Firebase services for leaderboard and analytics functionality. Firebase may collect basic device and usage information to help improve the game experience and keep leaderboard features working.

Ads

The game does not currently show ads.

How We Use Data

Leaderboard data is used only to display scores inside the game. We do not sell your personal data.

Data Removal

If you want your leaderboard name or score removed, contact us at samesayed20@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at samesayed20@gmail.com.
